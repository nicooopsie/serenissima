poetry by serenissima

it takes loss
to make a poet.

love comes in the form of your presence
and absence, unfortunately.

read the books a poem for you

three books. one thread.

we are not made of stone—
we respond, we echo, we become.

a poem for you

"grief is proof that love was there.
just a visitor bringing flowers
to pay respect to a love
that was once there."

the poet

writing so she can, at least, learn to let it go.

serenissima writes in the in-between — between holding on and letting go, between the love you give and the love you lose. her three books trace the full arc of a heart learning to carry itself.
